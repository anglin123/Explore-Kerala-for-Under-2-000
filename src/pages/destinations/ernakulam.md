<h2 id="introduction">Introduction</h2>

Ernakulam, often referred to as the twin city of Kochi, is the bustling commercial capital of Kerala. While it's known for its IT hubs and shopping malls, it is also home to the historic Fort Kochi and Mattancherry. For budget travelers, Kochi is incredibly friendly, offering cheap public transport (like the revolutionary Water Metro), affordable street food, and numerous free historical sights.

<h2 id="how-to-reach">How to Reach</h2>

- **By Train:** Ernakulam Junction (South) and Ernakulam Town (North) are major railway stations connecting to all parts of India.
- **By Bus:** The KSRTC bus stand offers budget-friendly rides from neighboring states and districts.
- **By Air:** Cochin International Airport (CIAL) is about 30km away. You can take the KSRTC AC low-floor bus (₹100) to the city center instead of expensive taxis.
- **Local Transport:** The Kochi Metro and the new Kochi Water Metro are the best and cheapest ways to get around. A Water Metro ticket to Fort Kochi costs just ₹20!

<h2 id="attractions">Best Attractions (Budget Friendly)</h2>

### Fort Kochi
Wander the cobblestone streets, see the iconic Chinese Fishing Nets, and explore colonial-era architecture. 
*Entry:* Free.

### Mattancherry Palace & Jew Town
Visit the beautiful Dutch Palace and stroll through the antique shops of Jew Town.
*Entry to Palace:* ₹5.

### Marine Drive
A picturesque promenade facing the backwaters. Perfect for an evening stroll. 
*Entry:* Free. Boat rides start at ₹100.

<h2 id="hidden-places">Hidden Places</h2>

- **Mangalavanam Bird Sanctuary:** A quiet green lung in the middle of the noisy city. Free entry.
- **Vypeen Island Beaches:** Take a Ro-Ro ferry (₹3) from Fort Kochi to Vypeen and take a cheap bus to Cherai or Kuzhupilly beach.

<h2 id="budget">Budget & Accommodation</h2>

### Where to Stay
Fort Kochi is a backpacker's paradise.
- **Hostels:** Zostel, goSTOPS, and Rampart Homestay offer dorm beds for ₹300 - ₹500 per night.
- **Budget Rooms:** Numerous homestays offer private rooms for ₹800 - ₹1200.

### Food
- **Street Food:** Try *Pazham Pori* (banana fritters) and beef fry at local thattukadas (street carts) for under ₹100.
- **Biryani:** Kayees Rahmathulla Hotel in Mattancherry offers legendary biryani for around ₹160.
- **Total Food Budget:** ₹300 - ₹400 per day.

<h2 id="itinerary">One-Day Budget Itinerary</h2>

1. **Morning (8:00 AM):** Take the Water Metro from High Court Terminal to Fort Kochi (₹20).
2. **9:00 AM:** Breakfast at a local eatery in Fort Kochi – Appam and Stew (₹60).
3. **10:00 AM:** Walk along the beach, see the Chinese Fishing Nets, and visit St. Francis Church.
4. **12:00 PM:** Walk or take a cheap auto to Jew Town. Explore the antique shops and the Paradesi Synagogue (₹5).
5. **1:30 PM:** Lunch at Kayees (₹160).
6. **3:00 PM:** Take the Ro-Ro ferry to Vypeen (₹3) and head to the beach, or return to Ernakulam via ferry (₹6) and relax at Marine Drive.
7. **Evening:** Enjoy sunset at Marine Drive with a cup of local *chaya* (tea - ₹15).

**Total Estimated Cost for the Day:** ₹600 - ₹800 (including hostel bed).
